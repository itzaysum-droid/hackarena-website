About the Event

Where speed meets innovation

HackArena is a high-energy hackathon by Ignite Room designed for makers, thinkers and builders who love turning ideas into working products. The experience is inspired by the motion, bold visuals and momentum of Subway Surfers.

This is not just about coding fast — it is about building smart, collaborating well and presenting solutions that feel exciting and relevant. The website uses a playful, neon-inspired UI to create an engaging event identity while still keeping the layout minimal, readable and senior-friendly.

Event Feel

Fast-paced. Creative. Vibrant. Competitive. The whole landing page is designed to communicate momentum, motion and modern hackathon culture.
